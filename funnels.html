What's going on here?


This page has no additional PostHog setup beyond adding the snippet on the header. Since PostHog has event autocapture out of the box, there is no need to send additional custom events to begin gathering insights through funnels and other tools.

In this case, actions were created from the autocaptured events (button clicks) and these actions were then used as steps on the funnel. The funnel being displayed here is a real PostHog funnel, which has been embedded into the page via our shared dashboards functionality.

As you go through the sign up process, your clicks to move onto the next step are recorded and reflected in the funnel. Please note that updates will not be immediate, so make sure to refresh the iframe after a few minutes to see the changes.

Another important comment is that PostHog funnels measure unique users. As such, once your events have been recorded, going through the sign up process again will not change the funnel (unless you did not previously complete the process). Currently, the funnel is set to a time range of "Last 48 hours", which means that it does not matter when your events were recorded within the 48 hours. If you do step 1 this morning and step 2 tomorrow afternoon, this will still count as a conversion in the funnel.
